# MCA Department Portal - Design Specification

## Overview
A modern, vibrant, and welcoming web portal for the MCA Department, designed to celebrate excellence and community. The aesthetic is clean, energetic, and highly readable, moving away from generic boilerplate layouts toward a custom, high-fidelity experience.

## Visual Identity
- **Primary Palette**: Professional Blues (#007AFF to #0051A8) for trust and stability.
- **Secondary Palette**: Soft gradients (Cyan to Violet) for "Today's Greetings" to create a festive and celebratory feel.
- **Background**: Soft off-white or very light blue tinted background (#F8FAFC) to reduce eye strain and make primary cards pop.
- **Typography**: 
    - **Headings**: Modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter or Montserrat) with semi-bold weights.
    - **Body**: Highly legible sans-serif with generous line height.

## Layout & Components

### 1. Header
- Sleek, minimal navigation with a clear logo placeholder.
- Primary CTA for "Public Portal" with a distinct button style.
- Subtle version indicator (e.g., v1.1) in the far right.

### 2. Hero Section
- Bold, centered welcoming message.
- Soft background gradient overlay to add depth without clutter.

### 3. Today's Greetings & Birthday Slideshow
- **Component**: A prominent, colorful card with a soft gradient background.
- **Functionality**: This section must display both department greetings (festivals, etc.) and a **dynamic slideshow for birthdays**.
- **Slideshow Details**:
    - If multiple students/faculty have birthdays, their names and photos should rotate in a smooth slideshow transition.
    - Festive iconography (balloons, confetti) should be integrated into the slideshow layout.

### 4. Department Calendar
- Two-column layout within the section for "Upcoming Events" and "Academic Schedule".
- Use clean icons and date badges for quick scanning.

### 5. Wall of Achievements
- Grid layout featuring high-quality portrait images of students and faculty.
- Each card includes a badge icon (trophy/medal), name, and a short description of the achievement.
- "Updated: Today" timestamp to maintain a sense of activity.

## Design Principles
- **Generous Whitespace**: Ensuring every element has room to breathe.
- **Soft Shadows**: Using deep, soft shadows (elevations) rather than borders to define sections.
- **Consistent Rounding**: All cards and buttons should have a consistent border-radius (approx. 16px to 24px) for a modern, friendly feel.
